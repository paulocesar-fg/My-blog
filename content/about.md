Hello! My name is Paulo César Fernandes Gama, and I’m passionate about technology and geek culture. This blog works as a kind of backup for my brain — a place to record my experiences with computing, mathematics, games, and other topics that spark my curiosity.

Gamma Writings focuses primarily on mathematics and computer science; however, it isn’t limited to those subjects. Here, I also share reflections on the geek universe in general and personal projects I'm developing along the way.

If you'd like to follow my routine more closely, I recommend checking out [my Instagram](https://instagram.com/paulocesar.fg). It’s important to mention that I only share content related to the topics mentioned above; my personal life and my family’s remain completely private.

This is a space to freely explore ideas and share experiences. So, dear reader, welcome to my blog — Gamma Writings.
